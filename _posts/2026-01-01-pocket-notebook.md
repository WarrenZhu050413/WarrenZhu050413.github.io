since the launch of ChatGPT. It's also the thing that is the easiest for
anyone else to do (you just need a pocket, a notebook that would fit inside
that pocket, and something to write with).

When I'm waiting for a bus, Uber, some person, my muscle to recover its
strength in the gym, or my poop to be displaced in the correct placr, I can
now do some doodling.
