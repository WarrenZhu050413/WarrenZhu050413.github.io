---
title: "Quantity of Information is a Horrible Metric"
date: 2025-12-02 23:26:49 -0500
---

I used to think quantity of information mattered. I would simply read more
and more, and I was better than most at this. Perhaps I’ve grown up—or
perhaps I’ve simply read enough—I’ve come to appreciate that a true
sentence is worth more than a mediocre book.
